ABOUT

What makes ghost/parasite different?

Not much. We like writing, but so do you. We take good work, but so do other mags. That being said, give us a read—you might like what you find.
If you need a gimmick, we give you the option of not needing to wait for our print edition to come out. Get your stories by mail or email, as soon as we've edited them. Hooray.

Short Stories

We like short stories, and so do you. That's why we pick the best ones and put them in our magazine. Browse our archives, or subscribe!

Quarterly

We release quarterly, so the best stories are always on their way to your doorstep. Premium subscribers also get a fifth issue as a holiday gift!

Early Access

A lot of you are fiction nerds, and can't wait to read new stories. If you want, we'll mail you paper copies of the stories as we're done editing them. You'll also get our print edition, of course!

Editing Services

We know writing. You probably do too, but if you want another set of eyes on yours, ask us about our editing services. We love reading new material, and we really love making it great.

SUBMISSIONS

From your brain to our eyeballs

You may be wondering what we're looking for in a story—mostly, we care that it's good. If you want to try and game the system, you can read our bios or past issues, but it might not help.

We have very few requirements for a submission. There is a form (below), and we prefer that any stories come through this portal, just for our convenience. We also ask that you keep your submissions down to 2 per quarter.

It's also very important to understand what you're giving away when you submit a story somewhere— we wrote our agreement to be very standard and mirror what other magazines are doing, but since this is often unclear, we wrote a lawyer version and a normal person version. We recommend that you read, at least, the normal person version. As always, feel free to reach out if you have any questions!
